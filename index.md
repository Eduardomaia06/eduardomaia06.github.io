## 🎤 How to Use
Once `cc-voice` is running, start using it by:

1. **Speaking Commands:** Say your command clearly into the microphone. 
2. **Listening to Responses:** Wait for the software to respond verbally.
3. **Adjusting Microphone Settings:** If needed, adjust your microphone settings for better reception. Check your computer’s sound settings to ensure the microphone is active.

## 📄 Voice Commands
Here are some basic voice commands to get you started:

- "Open [Application Name]" - Replace [Application Name] with the app you want to open.
- "What is the weather today?" - Get real-time weather information.
- "Translate [phrase] to [language]" - Use the voice interface for simple translations.

## 💡 Tips for Best Performance
- Speak clearly and at a moderate pace for the best recognition.
- Ensure your microphone is positioned correctly and is not too far away.
- Use a quiet environment to minimize background noise.
